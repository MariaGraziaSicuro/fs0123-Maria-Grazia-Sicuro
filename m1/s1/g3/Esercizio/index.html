Focaccia whit Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holyday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon, olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 1 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
